Scholarships

Diversity and inclusivity matter

Apply for a Scholarship

We are involved in many ways in order to build a better environment in tech for everyone. Two years ago we became Diversity Sponsor of The PHPDiversity Rainbow Elephpant campaign on Kickstarter when the project was seemingly about to end without making it. We are very proud that many others followed us and that the project reached its goal.

As we want to do more we are pleased to announce our Scholarships for phpDay 2020. We are committed to enabling as many people as possible to attend our conference, and to promoting an inclusive community; last year we introduced a ticket scholarship scheme (no travel) to encourage students, under-represented groups and those who can’t otherwise afford tickets to experience the event.

Preference will be given to members of under-represented groups in technology - women, minorities, LGBTQIA+, people with disabilities, or other under-represented groups; but consideration will be given to anybody who would otherwise be unable to attend without financial assistance.

These scholarships are made possible thanks to our Diversity Support Tickets initiative and the support received by GrUSP. If you want to help us and you’d like to get involved please contact us at info@grusp.org.

Application

You are invited to apply for a single conference ticket (no travel) for phpDay 2020. We will notify all applicants regarding the outcome of their application.

Contact

May you have any questions please don’t hesitate to contact us at info@grusp.org.
